Buyer pays via the seller unique BuySafely link. Funds are locked instantly in a secure Protocol vault. The seller receives the full agreed amount — always.
STEP 02

A unique HMAC-bound PoD token sent exclusively to the buyer via WhatsApp. Tied to that exact transaction — cannot be reused or faked.
